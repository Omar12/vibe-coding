Social Media Share Widget Example

This example demonstrates a social media share widget built with React. It includes:

  • Share on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and via Email
  • Copy link to clipboard functionality
  • Desktop overlay with social media options
  • Native share menu on iOS and Android devices

Demo image for sharing (via picsum.photos)

How it works:

  1. On desktop: Click the share button to open the overlay with social media options
  2. On mobile (iOS/Android): Click the share button to open the native share menu
  3. Copy the URL to clipboard by clicking the Copy button
